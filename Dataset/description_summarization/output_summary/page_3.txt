TITLE: Ordnance Survey: Navigating the role of AI and ethical considerations in geospatial technology

AUTHOR: Duncan MacRae

DATE: December 23, 2024

SOURCE: AI News (https://www.artificialintelligence-news.com/news/ordnance-survey-navigating-the-role-of-ai-and-ethical-considerations-in-geospatial-technology/)

SUMMARY:
- Manish Jethwa, CTO at Ordnance Survey (OS), discusses the potential of AI and machine learning (ML) in geospatial technology.
- Key benefits include:
    * Automatic feature extraction from imagery
    * Easier data query using natural language
    * Increased accessibility of geospatial data
- Challenges include:
    * Ethical considerations (bias, transparency)
    * Cybersecurity threats
    * Cultural resistance and change fatigue
- Importance of responsible AI development and workforce training is emphasized.

KEY POINTS:
- Advancements in AI and ML, particularly large language models (LLMs), will make geospatial datasets more user-friendly.
- Increased access to graphics processing units (GPUs) will enable training models for complex tasks.
- Ordnance Survey (OS) has a Responsible AI Charter to guide ethical integration of new technologies.
- Cybersecurity threats powered by AI are becoming more sophisticated, requiring comprehensive defense strategies.
- Companies must embrace change while managing risks and ethical considerations.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:
